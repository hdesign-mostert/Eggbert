Eggbert Eggs is a producer, packer and distributor of commercial and free range eggs focusing on major retail outlets in Gauteng and KZN

Welcome to Eggbert Eggs

Our commercial laying hens live in well ventilated hen houses, have good quality feed and water available at all times and are checked regularly to ensure they produce high quality eggs that we can be proud of. Eggbert guarantees its products to be free of antibiotics and hormones. When you buy Eggbert Eggs you can be rest assured that the product is safe and healthy. The eggs are transferred promptly to our packstations where they undergo a quality evaluation by trained QC personnel and are then graded and packaged into various packs to suit our customer's requirements. Eggs are delivered daily throughout Gauteng and KZN to stores that we service.
